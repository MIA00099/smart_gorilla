Our concept

Adventure in every bite.

Once upon a time, in a not-so-distant past, a greedy gorilla felt the call of adventure. He took with him his beloved pots and his precious ancestral recipes, because for our gorilla indulgence is not a sin, but an art. Thus, Smart Gorilla was born: a culinary adventure full of discoveries and surprises. Welcome to our gourmet jungle.
